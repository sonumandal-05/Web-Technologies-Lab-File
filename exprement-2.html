Famous Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is a symbol of love located in Agra.

Jaipur

Jaipur is known as the Pink City of India.

Goa

Goa is famous for its beaches and nightlife.

Kerala

Kerala is known for its backwaters and greenery.

Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal.

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest spiritual cities.

Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India.

Mumbai

Mumbai is the financial capital of India.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is famous for yoga and river rafting.

Ladakh

Ladakh is known for its mountains and cold desert.
